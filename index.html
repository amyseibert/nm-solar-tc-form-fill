Simplify Solar Tax Credit Docs

Complete, generate, and download NM state tax credit forms for your customers in minutes.

How it Works

Complete

Enter information about your customer and their installed system into our simple form fields.

Generate and Download

Our system instantly uses that information to fill out and download the required documents for the NM State Solar Tax Credit, including submission instructions for your customer.

Send

Send the completed forms to your customer, ready to be submitted! Make sure to send all 4 forms generated here. You will also need to send 1. copy of your customer's property tax bill, 2. copy of completed Electrical Permit, and 3. copy of approved Designs.

Next Steps For Your Customer

Send the documents to your customer and let them know to complete the following steps. It is always best for your customer to consult a tax professional!

Step 1

Go to the ECMD Submission Site and click "apply," then follow the steps in the "Instructions" document.

Step 2

Submit the form and all required documents. You should receive an email from ECMD confirming your submission. Keep an eye out for any communication from ECMD notifying you of further information needed.

Step 3

Once your application is accepted, the state of NM will send you a certificate of eligibility which you can then submit with form TRD-41406 when you file your taxes in the Spring.
