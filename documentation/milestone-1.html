PWP Milestone 1 ☀️

Purpose

This Project will be a website that allows the user to input data into designated fields

Stretch goal: The site will then generate documents using pre-made templates that are filled out using the data input by the user.

Audience

This website is designed for professionals in the solar industry, specifically those in charge of filling out the documents that must be prepared and sent to solar customers in order for them to be able to claim their New Mexico solar tax credit. Target audience: solar company owners, management, employees, and other industry professionals.

Goal

The primary goal of this site is to enable users to fill out key customer information in designated data fields in order to have the data saved in one place.

Stretch goal: This data, only entered once by the user, is then input into pre-made PDF templates of required tax documents for the New Mexico state solar tax credit, saving the user the time of having to fill out each form individually.

Site Use Scenario

This website will be used after a project is completed in order to complete the state tax credit documents for each customer.

Frustrations and Needs

Bill has two main problems when it comes to preparing the required state tax documents for his customers. First, he knows that company time and resources are currently being wasted on preparing the documents and that this will only get worse as his company continues to grow. His employees are all busy doing the jobs they do best so he has had to take over this task which wastes a minimum of 2 hours per project. Also, the format and exact requirements for the documents change often, requiring even more company time to be spent when documents need to be re-done if the customer doesn't submit them before new changes are made. Second, Bill knows that this is an area that is damaging his customer's experience with his company. Because there is a limited budget for the tax credit each year, documents must be submitted by the customer immediately after project completion to avoid missing out on the credit altogether. Bill knows that he needs a faster way to complete these documents in order to address these two problems.

Goals

Bill has one main goal: To cut down the 2 hours it currently takes to complete each customer's state solar tax credit documents as much as possible.

User Story

As an advanced user, I need a simple, well-thought-out website that will help me or my employees to complete the required state solar tax credit documents in under 2 hours per project.
